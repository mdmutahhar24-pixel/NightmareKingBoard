# Journal Entry 1

**Hours Logged:** 05:05:38.93

## Stage 1: Research
Before doing anything related to the keyboard, I wanted to research what types of items were the best for a gaming keyboard (since I wanted it for gaming). This is what I gathered:
- TKL keyboards are most preffered for gaming
- Gasteron Oil King Switches were good for gaming
- Most gaming keyboards had RGB lights

## Stage 2: Plan my Keyboard
I planned to make a keyboard based off <a href="https://hollowknight.fandom.com/wiki/Nightmare_King_Grimm">Nightmare King Grimm (NKG for short)</a> which is one of the coolest bosses in the game of Hollow Knight.
I did some additional Research to make my Keyboard theme match with NKG. This is what I gathered and updated my BOM with:
- Red and Black fit with NKG's style so I chose the Red and black BRED keycap set from Kraken Keycaps
- SK-6812 MINI-E RGB LEDs were chosed for the LEDs since it was preffered for keyboards
- Aluminum Sand Blasted Knurling Encoder Knobs (Red) to style my encoder
- Rotary Encoder for a custom touch
- Orpheus Pico (since Hack Club provided for it)

## Stage 3: Schematic
Since I had finished my BOM, I started with my PCB schematic. I had used about (I am not sure) 87 switches. I organized them into how a keyboard looks. Right now this is in progress since I am still working on wiring the matrix, working on the LEDs, and wiring the Rotary Encoder.
